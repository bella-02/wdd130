About Us

Always a romance lover, Bella grew up reading books which whisked her away to new worlds full of drama and swoon-worthy confessions of love. She wished to create a space which welcomed all romantics through its doors, so the Blushing Book was born. This store had its humble beginnings as a book van, traveling to different areas to offer an on-the-go book shopping experience. However, there was potential for so much more. In 2020, amidst the pandemic, Bella decided it was time for The Blushing Book to find a permanent home. After much hard work, the doors to this dreamy store were opened to the world on November 3rd, 2021, and the rest has been history.

The Blushing Book strives to be more than a store for its customers. We make book shopping into an experience. Comfortable lounge areas and many exciting events turns The Blushing Book into a place where readers can socialize with likeminded people. Once you’ve experienced the delightful atmosphere we offer, you will fall in love. Don’t believe us? Come visit and find out!
