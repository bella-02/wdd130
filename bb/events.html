Events

Monthly Book Club - 1st Wednesday @7:00 PM

Come join us at book club! The Blushing Book hosts a monthly book club every 1st Wednesday at 7:00 PM to discuss the month’s book. Everyone is welcome, no ticket purchase required. Refreshments will be available for purchase.

July’s Book Pick: Book Lovers by Emily Henry.

Spill-the-Tea Brunch - July 15th, 2023

Come mingle with fellow readers while enjoying delicious finger foods. Each admission ticket purchase includes one raffle ticket, with more available for purchase.

Ages 21 and up. Ticket price: $25 (food and drink included). There are a limited number of tickets so act quick! Email blushingbook@gmail.com or visit the store to purchase tickets.

Bi-Annual Writer's Seminar - January 3rd, 2024

Are you an aspiring author or simply love writing? Then you should join us at our upcoming Bi-Annual Writer’s Seminar, taught by author Stephanie Smith. Stephanie will be teaching about writing character development, and the struggles which come along with that. Bring your preferred tool for note taking and a readiness to learn!

Ticket price: $15. Ages 16 and up are welcome.
